The retrieval stack has three layers: SQLite FTS5 BM25 lexical search, local Model2Vec semantic similarity, and optional graph-aware expansion or reranking. Hybrid retrieval merges candidate sets with reciprocal-rank fusion or a documented equivalent, and each result must explain whether it came from lexical match, semantic match, or graph expansion.
